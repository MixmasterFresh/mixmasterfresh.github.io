ET Book Typeface

A Modernized Integration

The ET Book typeface offers a rich selection of weights and italics, including a semi-bold weight and a distinct display italic for headings. This typeface is designed for elegance and readability.

Bootstrap's typography utility classes integrate seamlessly with ET Book. For example, use .fw-bold for bold text, .fw-semibold for semi-bold text, and .fst-italic for italicized text. The display classes such as .display-1 and .display-3 make use of the display italic.

Oldstyle Figures

Oldstyle figures are used for text numerals, creating a harmonious flow in running text: 1234567890.
